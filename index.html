About Our Church

Christ Synagogue Ministries is led by Prophet Baraka David Ogillo in Arusha, Tanzania. We are dedicated to transforming lives through guidance, deliverance, and Spirit-filled worship—spreading the gospel and building a strong, loving community of believers through faith, discipleship, and prophetic ministry.

Our Mission

To glorify God and make disciples of all nations

Our Vision

A thriving community transformed by God's love and truth

Our Values

Faith, Integrity, Community, and Excellence

Our Services

Sunday Worship

Experience uplifting worship and powerful teaching of God's Word.

Prayer Meetings

Midweek prayer services for intercession, praise, and spiritual growth.

Prophetic Ministry

Experience the power of the Holy Spirit through prophetic words, divine guidance, and spiritual impartation.

Discipleship

Small group studies designed to deepen your faith and spiritual maturity.

Praise & Worship

Contemporary and traditional worship leading people closer to God.

Counseling

Pastoral and spiritual counseling to help you navigate life's challenges.
